<div>&lt;p&gt;To get started, let us identify the thread where the signal occurs.&lt;/p&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;p&gt;In a stack trace, there can be hundreds of threads running at the same time.&lt;/p&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;p&gt;&lt;span&gt;The relevant search information that needs to match the issue with a &lt;/span&gt;knowledge base article is present in the thread where the Operating System signal occurs.&lt;/p&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;p&gt;&lt;img width=&quot;100%&quot; src=&quot;https://97e791180fb2ccedbb75-dcbe5f8c57b31899abb7565ca1378165.ssl.cf2.rackcdn.com/Stack traces thread.jpg&quot; alt=&quot;&quot; /&gt;&lt;/p&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;p&gt;The first step is to identify the thread where the signal is triggered.&lt;/p&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;p&gt;To identify the thread, search the stack trace for words such as the following:&lt;/p&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;ul&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;li&gt;Signal&lt;/li&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;li&gt;Abort&lt;/li&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;li&gt;Signalhandler&lt;/li&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;li&gt;Handler and crash&lt;/li&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;/ul&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;p&gt;The following points may indicate that the stack trace is triggered by an Operating System signal.&lt;/p&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;p&gt;In the logs, the following error may be found:&lt;/p&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;p&gt;&lt;em&gt;FATAL ERROR : Caught a fatal signal or exception.&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/p&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;p&gt;&lt;em&gt;FATAL ERROR : Aborting the DTM process due to fatal signal or exception.&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/p&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;p&gt;&lt;em&gt;FATAL ERROR : Signal Received: &amp;nbsp;&amp;lt;and then the name and number of the signal&amp;gt;&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/p&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;p&gt;There might be different signals in the error.&lt;/p&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;p&gt;&amp;nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Example&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;p&gt;&lt;em&gt;SIGABRT 6 SIGBUS 10, SIGSEGV (11)&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/p&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;p&gt;&lt;em&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/p&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;p&gt;In the stack trace, an entry such as the following may be found:&lt;/p&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;p&gt;&lt;em&gt;&amp;lt;signal handler called&amp;gt;&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/p&gt;</div>
<div>&lt;p&gt;&amp;nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;</div>
